SHOP WITH...

Each month, MATCHESFASHION.COM works with a selected fashion insider or style influencer to curate the key looks of the moment as part of our Shop With… series. In this space, each featured stylist exclusively exhibits their hand-picked edit of hero pieces from our collections, creating 12 complete looks that showcase their unique style aesthetic. Look no further for fresh outfit inspiration.

View All

LA-based creative consultant and stylist Jayne Min epitomises effortless style. With an oversized yet minimalist aesthetic, her approach to fashion is refreshingly practical: ‘If I can’t eat or sleep or work in it comfortably, I’m not interested,’ she says.

Julie Sariñana is an LA-based blogger and founder of online website Sincerely, Jules – a snapshot of Sariñana’s style, travel adventures and home life. She describes her look as ‘California laid-back; it’s very easy, versatile, and comfortable.’

The Coveteur is the website that reveals the wardrobes of the world’s most stylish people. It was founded by Stephanie Mark, Jake Rosenberg and Erin Kleinberg as ‘a passion project to show the lives and personal style of the behind-the-scenes heroes of the fashion industry – the people whose opinions and stories shaped the looks we saw on the runway and red carpet,’ says Mark.
