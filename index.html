Each month, MATCHESFASHION.COM works with a selected fashion insider or style influencer to curate the key looks of the moment as part of our Shop With… series. In this space, each featured stylist exclusively exhibits their hand-picked edit of hero pieces from our collections, creating 12 complete looks that showcase their unique style aesthetic. ‘You should have a combination of things you hold on to and things you clear out – iconic pieces from designer collections or items that have a very special meaning should be kept.’

Stephanie Mark - The Coveteur

‘If I had to describe my style, I would say that it has personality. So say if it’s Saturday and I’m happy, I‘ll dress to reflect that. My personality is reflected in the outfit.’

Leaf Greener

‘I experiment with menswear and oversized garments. I never wear dresses or skirts on their own; they’re always layered over trousers or high boots.’

Margaret Zhang

‘I always fall in love with collections by Valentino, Raey, Gucci by Alessandro Michele and Joseph Altuzarra. Each one of these labels offers something distinctive for the modern woman.’

Tamu McPherson

‘Jeans are the easiest thing for me to style – they feel a lot like plain toast in that you get to accompany them however you want. I aim to create visual paradoxes. I never want to look like I’m dressed to fit in a box.’

Leandra Medine

‘I would say my style has evolved over the years but now I’m at a good place where I know what it is. It’s mainly feminine, classic and sophisticated with an edge – whether it’s a masculine touch or something more modern.’

Chriselle Lim

THIS MONTH: THE COVETEUR

The Coveteur is the website that reveals the wardrobes of the world’s most stylish people. It was founded by Stephanie Mark, Jake Rosenberg and Erin Kleinberg as ‘a passion project to show the lives and personal style of the behind-the-scenes heroes of the fashion industry – the people whose opinions and stories shaped the looks we saw on the runway and red carpet,’ says Mark.’

Here, The Coveteur’s team hand-pick their favourite pieces, styling 12 looks that showcase their take on the MATCHESFASHION.COM edit. Shop the looks below and follow the story at #MFxCoveteur.
