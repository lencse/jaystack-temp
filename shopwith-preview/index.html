Each month, MATCHESFASHION.COM works with a selected fashion insider or style influencer to curate the key looks of the moment as part of our Shop With… series. In this space, each featured stylist exclusively exhibits their hand-picked edit of hero pieces from our collections, creating 12 complete looks that showcase their unique style aesthetic. Look no further for fresh outfit inspiration.

‘I experiment with menswear and oversized garments. I never wear dresses or skirts on their own; they’re always layered over trousers or high boots.’

Margaret Zhang

‘I always fall in love with collections by Valentino, Raey, Gucci by Alessandro Michele and Joseph Altuzarra. Each one of these labels offers something distinctive for the modern woman.’

Tamu McPherson

‘You should have a combination of things you hold on to and things you clear out – iconic pieces from designer collections or items that have a very special meaning should be kept.’

Stephanie Mark - The Coveteur

‘If I had to describe my style, I would say that it has personality. So say if it’s Saturday and I’m happy, I‘ll dress to reflect that. My personality is reflected in the outfit.’

Leaf Greener

‘I always say less is more. It’s so cliché but it works every time. Try to always start with the piece that speaks to you and work around that.’

Jules Sariñana

THIS MONTH: SINCERELY JULES

Julie Sariñana is an LA-based blogger and founder of online website Sincerely, Jules – a snapshot of Sariñana’s style, travel adventures and home life. She describes her look as ‘California laid-back; it’s very easy, versatile, and comfortable.’ Here, Sariñana hand-picks her favourite pieces, styling 12 looks that showcase her take on the MATCHESFASHION.COM edit. Shop the looks below and follow the story on Instagram at @MATCHESFASHION #MFxSincerelyJules.
